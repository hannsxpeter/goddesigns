02 / Settlement

Settle at the bar, pay out before the van loads

Deal terms flow from the offer sheet into the settlement automatically. No back-office math at 1 a.m., no envelope of cash guesswork.

STL-02

Guarantee, split, or versus: the math is already done

Bandquarter compares the guarantee against the split in real time as box office numbers land. Taxes, facility fees, and expense caps are applied per your deal memo, line by line, with a paper trail.

  • Same-night ACH or instant payoutArtist gets a link, picks a method, money moves.
  • W-9 and tax docs collected up front1099s generated at year end without a scramble.
  • Signed settlement PDF, both partiesTour manager signs on a phone at the bar.
