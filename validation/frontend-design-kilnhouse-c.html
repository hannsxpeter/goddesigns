Kiln firing queue

A firing log that never misses a cone

Every kiln in the studio gets a live queue: what is loading, what is firing, what is cooling, and whose work is on which shelf. Members drop pieces on the shelf and see exactly when they come out.

  • Firing schedules by cone, with ramp, hold, and cool-down profiles saved per kiln
  • Shelf-by-shelf load maps so nothing gets fired at the wrong temperature
  • Automatic firing fees per piece, weighed once and billed straight to the member
