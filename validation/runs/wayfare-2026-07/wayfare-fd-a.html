02 / Flight and train tracking

Your legs, watched while you wander.

Every flight and rail leg on your itinerary is tracked live. Gate changes, platform swaps, and delays reach you before the departure board updates, along with what the delay does to the rest of your day.

  • Rail-first coverage. 60+ national rail networks, not just the airlines.

03 / Daily budget

Spend like a local, count like an accountant.

Set a daily number and log spends in three taps, in any currency. Wayfare converts on the day's rate, shows your pace against the plan, and tells you whether tonight is a tasting-menu night or a grocery-store picnic.

  • Pace, not guilt. Under yesterday rolls forward. Over yesterday spreads gently across the days left.
  • 160+ currencies with the mid-market rate captured at the moment you spend.
  • Split with a travel partner and settle once, at the end, in one number.
