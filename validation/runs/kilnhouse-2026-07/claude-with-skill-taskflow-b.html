Fair questions

Why is the core product free? +

Because paying to evaluate a project tool is backwards. Teams that outgrow three boards upgrade on their own. That conversion is our business model, stated plainly.

What happens when we pass 10 teammates? +

Nothing breaks. The eleventh invite prompts an upgrade to Pro. Until you accept it, everything you already have keeps working exactly as before.

Can we import from our current tool? +

Yes. TaskFlow imports CSV exports from Trello, Asana, and Jira, and maps columns to board lists during import. Your task history comes with you.

Who owns our data if we leave? +

You do. Export every board to CSV or JSON at any time, on any plan, including Free. Deleting your workspace removes it from our servers within 30 days.
