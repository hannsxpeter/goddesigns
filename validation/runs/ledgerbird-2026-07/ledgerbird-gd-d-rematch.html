Capture invoices without retyping them

Forward the email, snap the paper, or drop the PDF. Ledgerbird reads the client, the amount, and the due date, files the original next to the entry, and flags the invoices that go unpaid past their date.

When the deposit lands in your account, the invoice marks itself paid and your income line updates. No spreadsheet, no Sunday-night data entry.

Every swipe lands on the right Schedule C line

Connect the card you actually use. Software subscriptions, art supplies, the 50 percent meals rule: each expense arrives pre-sorted into its Schedule C category, and one tap confirms it.

Ledgerbird learns your pattern, so the vendor you tag once stays tagged. Come January, your deductions are already a list instead of a shoebox.
