Booking

Holds that behave like holds

First hold, second hold, challenge, confirm. Bandquarter keeps the ladder honest: when an agent challenges a date, the first hold gets the call and a clock, and the calendar updates the moment someone signs.

Offers, avails, and radius notes live on the date itself, so your talent buyer and your production manager read the same room, from the first email to load-in.

The split, settled before the band loads out

Ticket counts flow in from your ticketer, expenses are logged as they happen, and the deal terms were captured when the offer went out. By encore, the settlement is already done: review it with the tour manager, tap approve, and the payout is queued.

W-9s are collected at confirmation, 1099 totals accrue all year, and every settlement is archived against its show. No shoebox of night-of paperwork in March.
