Short answers for studio managers.

What does Kilnhouse keep together?

Kiln schedules, firing records, studio memberships, and operational communication live in one workspace instead of separate sheets and threads.

Can I manage more than one kiln?

Yes. Your signup starts with a kiln count so your workspace can reflect the equipment your studio manages.

What kinds of studios is it for?

Kilnhouse is designed for independent, cooperative, teaching, and community ceramics studios that need a shared operating picture.

What happens after I sign up?

We use your studio details to prepare the workspace, then send the next setup step to your work email.
