Third, the part everyone dreads.

Nobody withholds taxes from a freelancer's check, which is why the quarterly deadline hurts. Ledgerbird runs your actual income minus your actual deductions through the current federal and state self-employment rates and keeps a running estimate of what to set aside. It nudges you two weeks before each IRS due date, with the amount and the payment link.

An estimate, clearly labeled as one. Your accountant confirms the final number; you stop being surprised by it.
