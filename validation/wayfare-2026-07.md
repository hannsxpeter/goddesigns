# Validation round 4: Wayfare comparative, 2026-07-18

Third comparative round, first with the fully taste-updated skill (Subject test, hard substance gate, audience-fit trigger, all encoded from the owner's Ledgerbird rankings). Domain: "Wayfare, trip planning for independent travelers": rich subject-artifact world (tickets, itineraries, departure boards, budgets), attractor pull toward wanderlust photo-hero cliches. Matrix: 3 goddesign vs 3 frontend-design (official skill, goddesign rule waived) vs 1 no-skill control; same brief, host, prompt shape; all seven audited externally.

## Methodological confound, disclosed

This round's structured report added a "substance" field (where does the page show the product working). Reading that field before building likely nudged ALL runs, frontend-design's included, toward richer product artifacts than prior rounds. It applied equally to both skills, so within-round comparison is fair, but cross-round substance comparisons are confounded and no substance-improvement claims are made from round-to-round deltas.

## Convergence

- goddesign: three distinct directions and papers (brutalist light with imagery, industrial dark, Swiss light), three macrostructures, audits 3 of 3 green: third consecutive clean sweep. Honest footnote: gd-a and gd-c drew Archivo-superfamily rows (Archivo Black and Archivo 800 are rows 2 and 0 of the deck); a deck-diversification candidate is noted.
- frontend-design: concept variety was better this round (atlas / timetable / night board, all transit-flavored, but the subject itself is transit so that is fair), yet the font streak continued: Young Serif again (cumulative 6 of 9 fd runs across three rounds) and fd-b/fd-c both picked Bricolage Grotesque, keeping the pattern that two of three fd runs share a display font every round. Device recipe overlap persisted: tilted hero card mock in all three, marquee/ticker in two.
- Control: Fraunces again (second baseline in three to pick it), editorial-serif SaaS.

## Firsts

- **Organic imagery trigger.** gd-a's seeded Photographic macrostructure legitimately fired the imagery pipeline for the first time in any run: genimage produced a viaduct panorama, the run compressed 2.1MB PNG to a 119KB webp per the 200KB rule, embedded it once, used it as the direction states (re-cropped full-bleed bands), and disclosed "Photograph is a generated illustration" in the footer. Archived as wayfare-gd-a-viaduct.webp.
- **Substance gate, first round enforced:** all three goddesign runs shipped 3-4 labeled artifacts of use (tickets, boards, ledgers, itineraries); none shipped an assertion-only page. The gd-a page that the taste model predicts should rank highest pairs the boldest form with the densest substance.

## Owner verdict, recorded without spin

The owner judged that frontend-design beat goddesign IN EVERY CATEGORY this round, and separately that all runs of both skills still read as "typical Claude sites, all clean presentation". Two conclusions stand: (1) mechanical superiority (9 of 9 green audits, zero repeated identities across rounds, honest data) did not buy appeal; correctness and variance are necessary and insufficient. (2) The appeal gap is diagnosable: the fd pages ship an atmosphere layer (paper textures, tilted overlapping cards, glow, grain) that goddesign's restraint rules sanded off, leaving correct, distinct, schematic pages of flat bands and rectangular panels. Fixed same day: the atmosphere layer is now mandatory ground treatment exempt from the signature budget (SKILL.md 4c) with a matching boolean gate. The band-stack grammar investigation (separate forensics) addresses the deeper "all look alike" half.

## Verdict

Three rounds, three domains, one pattern: goddesign 9 of 9 externally-verified green with no repeated identity; frontend-design 0 of 9 with a persistent font attractor and shared device recipe; controls in catalogued attractors every time.
