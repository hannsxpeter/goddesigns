The itinerary is the plan, not a wish list

Lay out days, legs, and stays in one ordered list. Drag a city and every connection, booking window, and budget line downstream moves with it.

  • Reorder days and Wayfare recalculates every onward connection.
  • Attach bookings, confirmation codes, and notes to each leg.
  • Gaps and impossible connections get flagged before you book them.

Flights and trains on one board

Wayfare watches every leg you have booked: airline feeds and rail operator data in a single status column. A platform change in Barcelona shows up next to a gate change in Madrid.

  • Live delay, gate, and platform updates for air and rail.
  • A missed connection re-plans the rest of the day, not just the leg.
  • Works offline with the last synced state when you lose signal.

A daily cap, not a post-trip autopsy

Set a spending cap per day and log entries in seconds, in any currency. Wayfare converts on the day's rate and shows what is left before dinner, not after the trip.

  • Per-day caps with rollover: an underspent Tuesday funds a bigger Saturday.
  • Multi-currency entries converted at the day's rate.
  • End-of-trip export: every entry, leg, and rate as CSV.
